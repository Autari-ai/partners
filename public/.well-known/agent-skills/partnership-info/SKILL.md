# Partnership Information

Structured information about the Autari Partner Programme.

## Overview

Autari builds white-label automation workflows for domain experts. Partners bring their expertise, Autari builds the technology, and partners sell to their clients — keeping the majority of recurring revenue.

## How It Works

1. **Pay a one-time setup fee** — Choose your niche. Scope the automation together and agree on what the workflow will do.
2. **We build your workflow** — Autari designs and builds a white-label automation product ready for your clients.
3. **You sell to your clients** — Use your expertise and network. Set your own pricing. Own the relationship.
4. **You earn recurring revenue** — Keep the majority of every subscription your clients pay, plus royalties from the B2C pool. Forever.

## Earning Model

| Revenue Stream | Description |
|---------------|-------------|
| One-time setup fee | Pay once to have your workflow built. Then it's yours to sell. |
| Recurring subscription revenue | Your clients pay a monthly fee you set. You keep the majority of every payment. |
| B2C platform royalties | When platform subscribers use your workflow, you earn a share of their subscription too. |

## Benefits

- **Your own product** — A fully built automation workflow, based on your expertise, ready to sell.
- **No dev team needed** — Autari handles all the technology. You handle the relationships.
- **Scalability built in** — One workflow serves one client or one thousand. Same effort from you.
- **Majority revenue share** — You keep the lion's share of recurring client payments. Forever.
- **B2C royalty stream** — Earn passive income even when Autari sells your workflow directly on the platform.
- **Ongoing support** — Autari maintains and updates the workflow. You stay focused on growth.

## Setup Cost

Setup fees range from around £200 for a basic workflow to £2,000+ for advanced custom builds. You pay once, then earn from it indefinitely.
